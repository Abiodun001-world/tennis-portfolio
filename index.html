More About Me

On the tennis court, Abiodun's fluid style and graceful strokes reflect the elegant biomechanics he has studied in flora and fauna. His easy power and versatile game have all the marking of an athlete utterly in tune with his environment. Abiodun's ambitions are to reach the upper echelons of professional tennis while also sharing his unique perspectives on movement, feel, and the mind-body connection. In addition to his playing pursuits, Abiodun has embraced coaching as a way to cultivate the same appreciation for the inherent beauty of the game in others. Drawing insights from the efficient mechanics of a dolphin's swimming motion or a tree's root structure, his intuitive and holistic coaching philosophy focuses on developing complete athletes and human beings. More than just groundstrokes and strategy, Abiodun works with students of all ages to nurture their athleticism through environmental awareness and by harnessing the regenerative forces of the natural world. His creative approaches, whether having a young player shadow the footwork of a gazelle or consider the spin geometry of a whirling dervish, unlock new pathways for athletic growth.
